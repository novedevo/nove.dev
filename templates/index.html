About Me

I'm a computer engineering student located in Vancouver, BC and studying at Simon Fraser University. My interests include music, hiking, coding and video games. I love learning new things and working on accessibility. In my spare time, I like solving puzzles such as nonograms, shikaku, and crosswords. My passion projects include music production, game design, home network architecture in the Unifi ecosystem, and home server architecture using Docker & Kubernetes under Debian (formerly FreeBSD) with TrueNAS. I have experience in Java, Rust, C++, Python, HTML/CSS/JS, and hardware design using VHDL and Quartus. I've built various projects with Arduino, ESP8266, and Raspberry Pi, and I've worked with AutoCAD and Inventor.
